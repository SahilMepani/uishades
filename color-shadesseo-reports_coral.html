Notes

Coral is the warm pink-orange named after the living reef organism. The CSS value is brighter than what most coral actually looks like underwater but matches the popular interior-design tone that took off in the 2000s and again in 2019 as Pantone's color of the year. Designers reach for it for energetic accent buttons, calls to action, and editorial palettes that want warmth without aggression. Against white the contrast is borderline for body text and reliable for headlines. Deep navy or charcoal handles type-heavy layouts; the contrast between cool dark type and warm coral surface reads as intentional rather than accidental.
